Follow these steps

  1. In a kadai add the oil and then add mustard seeds, and let them sizzle.
  2. Once sizzled add cumin seeds, followed by the Asafoetida, curry leaves curry leaves.
  3. Then add the rice and dal, and mix well.
  4. Once mixed add the ka shmiri red chilli powder, turmeric powder, coriander cumin powder, goda masala and salt and mix well.
  5. Add the hot water, and mix well.
  6. Cover and cook on a medium flame for 10 minutes.
  7. After 10 minutes check if the rice is cooked and the water has evaporated.
  8. Garnish with some ghee, fresh cilantro and grated fresh coconut.
